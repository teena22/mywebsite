Imaginit

Imaginit means Imagine it. Imaginit is a Python based Image Editor. It allows us to apply several effects on the image. It also allows fipping, resizing and rotating the Image. I used Pillow to perform various image operations and OpenCV for denoising the Image.

Source code: https://github.com/teena22/imaginit
